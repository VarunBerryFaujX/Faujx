01 · The problem, precisely

Four costs your hiring process is paying today.

None of them show up as a line item. All of them compound across every open req.

Pain 01 · Time destruction
Your senior engineers are your most expensive screening infrastructure.
Every hour spent on a candidate who never had a shot is an hour not building product. Multiply by every open req running in parallel. FHOS: nothing unqualified reaches an engineer.
Pain 02 · The #120 applicant
Your best candidate isn’t absent. They’re buried.
A manual process can’t give applicant #120 the same look as #5 — reviewer hours run out, and arrival order decides instead of merit. FHOS: every applicant processed at the same bar, whether a req draws 12 or 200+.
Pain 03 · No consistent bar
What “strong senior engineer” means depends on who interviewed that week.
That’s not a bar — that’s variance. And in a regulated PBM, undocumented judgment is also a governance gap. FHOS: one role-calibrated rubric, every candidate, every reviewer — with an audit trail.
Pain 04 · The mis-hire tax
A senior mis-hire is a six-figure mistake decided in a 45-minute interview.
Salary, ramp time, team disruption, backfill — and no structured signal until five rounds are already spent. FHOS: the bulk of the evidence exists before live interview time is committed.
02 · What FaujX is

A Hiring Operating System. Not an agency. Not another ATS.

FaujX (FHOS) is a capability-validation layer that sits above the hiring stack SmithRx already runs. It doesn’t source candidates, doesn’t replace recruiters, and doesn’t touch your final loop. It takes the applicants you already have and converts them into structured, evidence-backed, decision-ready signal — before anyone spends live interview time. We call it The Un-Interview.

03 · How it works

Seven stages. Every applicant runs all of them.

One funnel, context carried end to end, no calls or scheduling at any stage. Gaps don’t eliminate a candidate — they carry forward as probes for the next stage.

01
Ingestion & Normalization
Every applicant parsed into one structured profile, regardless of source.
02
Capability Intelligence
Capabilities extracted and scored against the benchmark you define for the role.
03
Adaptive Screening
Role-calibrated assessment that adapts to candidate performance in real time.
04
Role Challenge
Optional
Technical sandbox or business case, matched to the role.
05
Structured AI Interview
Built-in interview environment — recorded, transcribed, scored.
06
Expert Human Review
Domain experts review every output on a structured rubric. The governance layer.
07
Decision-Ready Report
Evidence, gap analysis, recommendation, 0–12 month ramp. ATS updated.

Five human-in-the-loop checkpoints — your hiring managers stay in control. ~80% of the hire / no-hire evidence exists before any live interview.
